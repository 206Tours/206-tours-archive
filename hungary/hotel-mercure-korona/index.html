Hotel Mercure Korona ****
1053 Budapest, Kecskeméti utca 14., Hungary

About the Hotel Mercure Korona:

Located in the heart of the city center 4 star hotel Mercure Budapest Korona, opposite the National Museum and a few minutes walk from the river Danube.

Direct acces to metro station (Kálvin square) at the entrance of the new Main street of Budapest.

Hotel Mercure Budapest Korona is perfect for weekend or business travel. Congress capacity up to 120 people, WiFi internet, high-speed internet,Privilege floor, pool, sauna, garage, two restaurants, lobby bar, souvenir shop, friendly staff.
