About the Hyatt Regency Paris Etoile:

Hyatt Regency Paris Etoile, formerly the renowned Hotel Concorde La Fayette, is 0.9 mi from both the Arc de Triomphe and the famous Champs Elysées.

The designer guest rooms each include a desk, cable TV, air conditioning and a safe. All rooms at the Hyatt Regency Paris Etoile offers views over the Paris. Deluxe bath products are provided in the private bathrooms.

This hotel is connected to the Palais des Congrès and La Defense business district is only 5 minutes away by Metro. A 24-hour concierge, currency exchange and a business center are among the additional services. Porte Maillot Metro Station is an 8-minute walk away and the airport bus shuttles to Charles de Gaulle Airport are located right outside the hotel.

Meetings & Events

When it comes to full-service event planning, Hyatt Regency Paris Étoile is in a league of its own. Linked to the Palais des Congrès convention centre, the hotel offers the biggest meeting venue in Paris and has the experience, expertise and know-how to plan and execute extraordinary events with outstanding service and attention to detail.

With 2800 sq m divided into 35 seperate event spaces, our outstanding facilities, newly-refurbished with a bright, modern & inspiring design and state-of-the-art technology, ensure your event is seamless, memorable and stress-free. Whether you are planning a grand gala event, hosting a private VIP dinner or celebrating a personal occasion, our expert team will answer each of your need.

Business travellers will appreciate our proximity to La Défense business district and main Parisian attractions, as well as our direct access to public transportation and a mall.
