About the Sofitel Paris La Defense:

10 mins from the Champs-Elysées and the CNIT-the foremost European business center-the Sofitel Paris La Défense is a contemporary 5-star hotel with a quiet, discreet atmosphere that is ideal for work and relaxation. Each of the rooms and suites is synonymous with calm and space. The overall harmony stems from the natural simplicity of the materials and the brown and gray tones used, uplifted by bright splashes of color. Let us introduce you to a whole new world of luxury and modernity. Special features Discover the only 5-star hotel in La Défense, offering a sophisticated and enjoyable atmosphere.

Rooms

Our 151 elegant rooms and suites boast comfortable MyBeds and luxury bathrooms. Modern in design, all rooms feature satellite television as well as WiFi and conventional internet access (complimentary in Luxury rooms). Our video-on-demand service is available in every room, along with games on a flat-screen TV.

Classic Room - large double bed or twin beds

258 sq. ft. (24 m²), free WIFI, exclusive Mybed bedding, opening window, minibar, tea and coffee making facilities, 48" HD TV, Apple TV, Bose speaker, safe, bathtub, Hermès products. Connecting rooms and twin beds available on request.

Superior Room - 1 large double bed

280 sq. ft. (26 m²), free WIFI, exclusive Mybed bedding, opening window, minibar, tea and coffee making facilities, 48" HD TV, Apple TV, Bose speaker, safe, rain shower, Hermès products. Connecting rooms available on request.

Superior Room - twin beds

280 sq. ft. (26 m²), free WIFI, exclusive Mybed bedding, opening window, minibar, tea and coffee making facilities, 48" HD TV, Apple TV, Bose speaker, safe, rain shower, Hermès products. Connecting rooms available on request.

Luxury Room - large double bed or twin beds

301 sq. ft. (28 m²), free WIFI, exclusive Mybed bedding, opening window, minibar, Nespresso machine, 48" HD TV, Apple TV, Bose speaker, safe, bathtub and separate shower, Hermès products, gym kit. Twin beds available on request.

JUNIOR SUITE - large Double Size Bed, single sofa bed + additional bed; free parking

377 sq. ft. (35 m²), contemporary design, lounge area, free WIFI, exclusive Mybed bedding, opening window, minibar, Nespresso machine, 55" HD TV, Apple TV, Bose speaker, safe, bathtub and separate shower, Hermès products, gym kit.

PRESTIGE SUITE - large Double Size Bed + additional beds; top floor, free parking

710 sq. ft. (66 m²), contemporary design, separate bedroom and lounge, exclusive Mybed bedding, opening window, minibar, Nespresso machine, 55" HD TV, home cinema, Apple TV, Bose speaker, safe, separate bath and shower, Hermès products.
