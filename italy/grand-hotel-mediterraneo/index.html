Grand Hotel Mediterraneo ****
Lungarno del Tempio 44, Lungarno del Tempio, 50122 Florence, Italy

About the Grand Hotel Mediterraneo:

The Grand Hotel Mediterraneo has a wonderful view over the famous river Arno banks (called "Lungarni"), near the beautiful Piazza Santa Croce, with a stunning panorama of the city.

The Grand Hotel Mediterraneo is very close to the most important monuments, including Piazza Santa Croce, Piazzale Michelangelo, the Uffizi Gallery and Ponte Vecchio.

Rooms

The 329 rooms at Grand Hotel Mediterraneo are large, luminous and equipped with all comforts. They have been designed and refurbished with great care in all details. All of the Superior rooms have wide windows to make the best of the magnificent view over the river Arno and the Tuscan hills. Non-smoking rooms are also available upon request.

All classic rooms are located on the 1st, 2nd, 3rd and 4th floor of Min Building. They have parquet floors and marble bathroom.

They can be single, double, triple and quadruple.

In the relaxed and quiet atmosphere of the Grand Hotel Mediterraneo, the chefs of the three in-house restaurants - the Taverna, the Ristorante Arno and the Ristorante Fiesole - are ready to welcome you to let you try the delicious specialties of the traditional local cuisine. The American Bar and Lounge is the right place to enjoy a wide choice of drinks before or after dinner.

La Arno

In the morning the Arno dining room offers an excellent breakfast, consisting of a large selection of sweet and savory products with a gluten free (AIC) option provided in a separate area. The wide variety of the buffet meets the needs of those who follow particular medical, ethical or religious diets.

The à la carte menu for lunch and dinner offers dishes made with the freshest of ingredients and includes the famous Florentine T-bone steak, a particularly precious cut of meat obtained from the Chianina breed of cattle.

La Taverna

Chef Ragoni, with years of Italian and international experience, focuses his cooks on the careful selection of the finest and freshest of ingredients to present the most authentic tastes of Italian cuisine in true Tuscan tradition. His fastidious cooking methods enhance the taste and texture of the selected ingredients as well as meet the needs of those who prefer healthy nutritious food.

The cozy, private ambience of the La Taverna restaurant is perfect for organising birthdays and small private parties.

La Fiesole

For galas, banquets or convivial dinners during events organised at the Globo Congress Center, the elegant, bright environment of the Fiesole Restaurant is the ideal choice for entertaining guests and delighting their palate.

Lounge Bar

The Lounge Bar is the perfect place to have an aperitif, sip cocktails and drinks, eat pizza and sandwiches, but also taste our chef's dishes in a pleasant and informal setting.

The hotel's restaurants provide 24-hour service upon request to reception for non-regular time slots with fast-food recommendations.

The restaurant, in line with the philosophy of the hotel, accepts small and medium-sized pets.

Breakfast is served from 6:30 until 10:00 am. The restaurant is open from 12:00 to 2:00 pm and again from 7:00 to 10:00 pm.

MEETINGS

Globo is the congress centre within the Grand Hotel Mediterraneo which includes 15 meeting rooms, most with natural light, in recently refurbished spaces and equipped with state of the art technology, perfect for organising small and large corporate events, congresses, conferences and to showcase product launches.

At your disposal is our efficient Optical Fiber Wi-Fi network with speeds of up to 100 Mbps. The fast Wi-Fi allows up to 500 total users and 300 simultaneous users to connect at once. The Access Point Dual Band device allows 100 users to be connected to each individual device (Ruckus) at the same time.

The facilities available in each room include:

  • video projector and screen
  • audio system
  • microphones
  • flipcharts
  • fax/photocopying

Our Event Office is available for any additional needs, including equipment or technical assistance.

The 3,380 sqft (314 sqm) Salone dei Continenti on the ground floor and the 2368 sqft (220 sqm) Salone degli Oceani on the first floor can each be subdivided into 3 separate rooms.

One of the entrances to the convention center allows quick and easy access for larger equipment and automobiles, for exhibitions and automotive events.

We complete our congressional offer with personalised catering services with dishes created by our chef Andrea Ragoni, accompany you throughout the day; from the welcome coffee and cooking displays to the gala dinner grand finale.
