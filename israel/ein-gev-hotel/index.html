About the Ein Gev Hotel:

The Ein-Gev holiday resort is situated on the eastern shores of the Sea of Galilee and is one of the largest in the area.

The resort focuses mainly on organized tours and vacations for tourists from all over the world.

Our 166 rooms are suitable for couples, families and groups, and their location on the shores of the lake provides an ideal setting for a relaxing and enjoyable vacation in a beautiful clean environment.

Over the years the name Ein-Gev has been synonymous with professional service and quality products; we strive to maintain that reputation and continue to provide the best for our clients.

Many of our guests choose to return time and again to enjoy a pampering, stress-free vacation in the enchanted lakeside resort.

We are here to make sure you get the best service available – always.

Prices inlude bed and breakfast.

Restaurant

The dining room, which seats up to 300, is located in the main building with a view to the lake. Meals are served in an open buffet style. Rich, typical Israeli breakfasts served daily at convenient times for all our guests. In the evenings the dining room serves as a restaurant where you will find an open buffet catering a variety of salads, meat dishes, side dishes, soup and desserts. The dining room can be reserved for private parties/ events by prior arrangement. Our staff is at your service and will be glad to assist in any way.

Bar

As evening comes, and the bar opens you can sit down and relax in front of a large screen to watch music clips and sporting events. You can order anything from soft drinks and quality coffee to imported or locally produced alcoholic beverages. The bar can be reserved for private events by prior arrangement.

Conference Halls

The resort has 3 conference halls:

  • The Kineret Hall – up to 40 people
  • The Galil Hall – up to 45 people
  • The Golan Hall – up to 45 people

It is recommended to reserve the hall you want in advance of your event, due to limited availability at certain times.

Guests of the resort will enjoy free parking close to the rooms.

Treatment Rooms

The Ein-Gev Holiday Resort boasts two treatment rooms where you can enjoy a variety of treatments performed by a professional and experienced team. The rooms are near the reception where you can get information about the various treatments available and make a reservation. The staff at the treatment rooms know you are on vacation and will ensure you feel great when you leave.

Beach

The Ein-Gev Holiday Resort is situated on one of the most beautiful beaches on the Sea of Galilee. The beach is long and wide and covered in wonderful natural sand.

During the season, which starts at around April, you will find on the spacious beach luscious lawns and deck chairs, swings and a kiosk selling a variety of items to make your experience pleasurable.

Entrance to the beach is restricted to our guests only, therefore offering more privacy. There is a lifeguard on duty during bathing hours.

Outdoor Sports

Sportsman and those who wish to exercise will find a wide selection of outdoor sports facilities. They are located in a lovely garden, overlooking the Sea of Galilee on the south side of the resort.

Games Room

Situated in the dining room building, the games room is all about family fun and enjoyment.

There, you will be greeted by an experienced guide who will offer a variety of activities for different ages (children under 5 must be accompanied by an adult):Sony Playstation, computer games, board games, arts and crafts activities,and for the younger ones – ball pool, doll-houses, carpentry table and tools, reading and story-time area.

The games room is open daily in season (July-August, Jewish Holidays) and on most weekends in the mornings and early afternoons.

16-Plus

In a hall located by the reception you will find a pool table ready to use and enjoy and a tennis table where you can practice and improve your skills.

Equipment for these games can be found at the reception desk – just come, leave an I.D and have fun free of charge.

A family tournament or friendly competition could enhance your holiday experience!

Playground

At the entrance to the Holiday Resort, near the dining room, you will find a playground for the little ones. The playground offers a stopping point for parents and children on their way to and from the dining room. All facilities are safe if used properly.

Gift Shop

At the Gift Shop you’ll find a large selection of souvenirs and gifts, knickknacks, jewelry, cosmetics, and other products.

Tourists from abroad are eligible for Tax-Exemption Purchases on products.

The shop is operated by a multilingual and experienced staff.

Laundry Room

In the laundry room you’ll find coin-operated washers and dryers. Irons are available at the Reception Desk.
