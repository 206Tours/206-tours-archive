Trinity Capital Hotel ***
Pearse Street, D2 Dublin, Ireland

About the Trinity Capital Hotel:

Trinity City Hotel is a 262 room, stylish hotel in the heart of Dublin City Centre, just a few minutes stroll from Grafton Street, O'Connell Street, Temple Bar and St Stephens Green. Our ideal location, on Pearse Street, Dublin 2, allows for a peaceful stay while still being close to the action.

The world-famous Trinity College is on our doorstep, while the IFSC, Dublin Convention Centre, Dublin Castle, the Silicon Hub and Grand Canal Docks are only a short walk away. Main shopping areas of Grafton Street and Henry Street are close by, while Dundrum Town Centre is just a Luas ride away.

The most central four star hotel in Dublin, Trinity City Hotel offers contemporary comfort, complimentary 200MB superfast WiFi and delicious modern dining at the Courtyard Restaurant, Brunswick Bar and Courtyard Garden. Our Concierge is available daily from 7:00am to 11:00pm and room service is available 24 hours.

With 262 Guest Bedrooms at Trinity City Hotel, ranging from Classic, Superior, Deluxe and Executive, to our striking Executive Suites and Georgian Suites, we offer stylish, relaxing surrounds for business or leisure. Enjoy our Buffet Breakfast, including both a hearty full Irish and continental style.

Old meets new at Trinity City Hotel, where original Georgian features and historically unique buildings mix with stunning art work, adventurous colour schemes and deluxe furnishings. Exposed brick walls, high ceilings and vintage glamour make Trinity City Hotel a special place.

Trinity City Hotel is steeped in history; our charm is down to the modern chic versus magical vintage feel of the hotel. Made up, in parts, of original Georgian buildings, a former fire station and featuring a courtyard garden, Trinity City Hotel is an eclectic mix of contemporary comfort.

Situated on Dublin's historically important Pearse Street, the site dates back to Viking times. Today, Trinity City Hotel derives much of it's character from the buidings' historical past, once home to Tedcastles Coal Merchants, the British Army recruiting office and Dublin Fire Brigade.

Ireland's 1916 rising hero Padraig Pearse was born just doors away and part of the hotel occupies the garden where Padraig and his brother Willie once worked and played. Trinity City Hotel retains many fascinating Georgian features, including the old shop front of our Number 26 Suite.

COURTYARD RESTAURANT

Looking for somewhere to enjoy a pre-theatre meal, a romantic treat, or a group night out? Trinity City Courtyard Restaurant is the perfect place to relax, for lunch or dinner. An exciting mix of contemporary and traditional dining, Trinity City Courtyard Restaurant is ideal for any special occasion.

Trinity City Courtyard Restaurant is wonderfully comprised of distinctive and unique areas within the hotel – there is front lounge with panoramic windows overlooking Pearse Street, Piano Lounge which is perfect for mid-day lunch meetings, or Courtyard Garden area which is relaxed oasis of green in the heart of city centre.

We invite you to enjoy both restaurant and bar menu in any of these areas.

BRUNSWICK BAR, TERRACE & LOUNGE

The Brunswick Bar is a most popular spot for a quiet drink, a quick coffee or a casual bite. The bar is a chic mix of old school charm and contemporary design, with original georgian features, ornate mirrors and a cosy, welcoming bar with high leather chairs.

The Brunswick Lounge, facing out onto Pearse Street, with impressive panoramic windows, is the perfect location for an informal business meeting or catching up with friends over tea and cakes. Why not enjoy our Brunswick Terrace, on a fine day; relax and watch the word go by.

Choose from our morning menu or bar lunch menu; fresh pastries, asian style pulled pork wrap, Trinity triple decker BLT, open crab sandwich, homemade soup of the day.

Bar menu is served from 12:00pm to 10:00pm, Monday to Sunday; with pastries and other breakfast favourites in the morning, and finishing off with Fish and Chips, 8oz Irish Beef Burger, Cajun Chicken Caesar Salad or the Piri Piri Irish Chicken Burger with a Charcuterie Board to start and a Praline Crème Brulee later in the day.
