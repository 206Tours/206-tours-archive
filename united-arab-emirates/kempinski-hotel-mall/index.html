Kempinski Hotel Mall *****
Sheikh Zayed Road, Al Barsha, Dubai, United Arab Emirates

About the Kempinski Hotel Mall:

In the heart of Dubai is the 5-star Kempinski Hotel Mall of the Emirates. Connected to Mall of the Emirates, guests can enjoy easy access to over 700 shops, special deals with and many Ski Dubai, Magic Planet IFLY, Little explores, ORBI in Mirdiff City Center can be booked prior/post arrival. 393 rooms and suites offering spectacular views of Dubai’s skyline. 20 stunning Aspen Chalets overlooking the incredible snowy slopes of Ski Dubai.

Ideally located on Sheikh Zayed Road with easy access to the Metro, Kempinski Hotel Mall of the Emirates is the perfect starting point to discover Dubai’s beaches, golf courses and Dubai's Parks and Resorts. Downtown Dubai and Burj Khalifa are also only minutes away.

There are many dining choices at the hotel. Try authentic Levantine cuisine at Olea, Spanish tapas at Salero, and drinks at Noir.

For business travelers, our world-class meetings and events facilities will impress with 17 event venues and exclusive limousine transfers with in-car Wi-Fi.

OLEA

Offering a new take on Arabic cuisine, Olea is a contemporary modern Levantine restaurant which celebrates the tastes, ingredients and aromas from the six nations – Syria, Palestine, Jordan, Southern Turkey, Lebanon and Cyprus.

From the live open kitchen, Olea’s chefs serve up an authentic experience with an array of freshly prepared dishes, from the El Homos Beiruti of Lebanon and Nayeh (raw meat) selection of Syria to the Sultan Ibrahim of Cyprus and Soft Kunafa of Palestine.

Shisha is served daily on The Olea Terrace from noon until late
